Helpful Links

The official website for the New Castle County Coaches Cross Country and Track Association (N5CTA)

N5CTA.com

This is the official website for N5CTA, the New Castle County Coaches Cross Country and Track Association. The site contains a calendar of invitationals (many of which are attended by Concord XC and many of which are not), recaps of meets, and results from the meets. It also has results from many previous seasons, as well as maps for some of the courses.

N5CTA.com Course Maps

The official website for all Concord sports

RaidersSports.com

This is the official website for all sports at Concord High School. Schedules and results can be viewed for all sports (although only tri-meet results are generally posted - refer to N5CTA.com for invitational results).

RaidersSports.com Cross Country Page

A database of cross country and track & field results past and present

de.MileSplit.com

This website has a huge amount of information for and about runners. It gives past and current runners' season bests and personal bests, and the results from many invitationals are posted here as well, although N5CTA.com is faster in releasing results.

A database of cross country and track & field results past and present

Blue Hen Photography

Blue Hen Photography contains a huge number of pictures from many sporting events, including cross country meets, taken by photographer Geoff Heath. The albums are very extensive, making it a great source for seeing runners in the heat of a race.
